# VaniHealthBot 🌸

Your holistic personal health & fitness Telegram bot — built for body recomposition,
the half marathon, and a lean, strong, pilates-princess physique.

---

## Features

- 📸 **Food photo calorie estimation** — snap and log meals
- 📊 **PICOOC scan OCR** — auto-extract all body composition metrics
- 🥗 **Personalised meal plans** — Singapore-friendly, high protein
- 🛒 **Meal prep guide + shopping list**
- 💪 **Freeletics-style workout plans** — adapts to your equipment + time
- 🧘 **Pilates integration** — cycle-aware, recovery-focused
- 🏃‍♀️ **Half marathon training plan** — with Singapore race calendar
- 🏅 **IPPT + functional fitness benchmarks**
- ❤️ **Heart rate zone training** — Zone 2 for fat burn, Zone 4 for HIIT
- 🌸 **Menstrual cycle sync** — adapts workouts & nutrition to your phase
- 🔄 **Macro cycling** — different targets on workout vs rest days
- 📈 **Google Sheets sync** — all data auto-logged
- 🇸🇬 **Singapore-specific** — local foods, routes, race calendar
- 💬 **AI coach** — science-backed, warm, always available
